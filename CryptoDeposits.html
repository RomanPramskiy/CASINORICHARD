Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common cryptocurrency deposit questions

Are cryptocurrency deposits safe at online casinos?

Yes, crypto deposits are generally very safe when using licensed and reputable casinos. Blockchain technology provides transparent, immutable transaction records. However, always verify the casino's reputation and licensing before depositing.

Do crypto deposits have fees?

Most casinos don't charge fees for crypto deposits, but you'll pay network transaction fees (gas fees) to the blockchain. These vary by cryptocurrency - TRON/TRX typically has the lowest fees (under $1), while Ethereum can be higher during network congestion.

How long do crypto deposits take to process?

Most crypto deposits are processed within 10-60 minutes, depending on network congestion and required confirmations. TRON and USDT (TRC20) are typically fastest (1-5 minutes), while Bitcoin can take longer during busy periods.

Can I withdraw to a different wallet than I deposited from?

Most casinos allow withdrawals to different wallet addresses for security reasons. However, some may require withdrawals to the same address for anti-money laundering compliance. Check the casino's specific policies before depositing.

What happens if I send the wrong cryptocurrency?

Sending the wrong cryptocurrency to a wallet address can result in permanent loss of funds. Always verify you're sending the correct crypto to the right network. Contact casino support immediately if you make a mistake - some recoveries are possible but not guaranteed.
