Yes, Aviator uses a provably fair system with cryptographic algorithms. Each round's outcome is determined by a random number generator that players can verify independently. The game has a 97% RTP rate, making it one of the fairest crash games available.

What's the maximum win possible in Aviator?

The maximum multiplier in Aviator can theoretically reach very high numbers (100x, 1000x or more), but such events are extremely rare. Most casinos set a maximum win limit per round, typically around €100,000-€500,000 depending on the operator.

What's the best cashout strategy for beginners?

For beginners, we recommend the conservative strategy with auto cashout set between 1.2x-1.5x. This gives you a high win rate (70-80%) and helps you learn the game mechanics without risking large losses. As you gain experience, you can experiment with higher multipliers.

How long does each Aviator round last?

Each Aviator round is unpredictable in length. Some rounds may crash almost immediately at 1.0x, while others can last much longer with high multipliers. On average, most rounds last between 8-40 seconds, with 20 seconds of betting time between rounds.

Can I play Aviator on mobile devices?

Yes, Aviator is fully optimized for mobile devices. You can play on smartphones and tablets through your casino's mobile website or dedicated app. The game maintains all features and smooth gameplay across all devices.
