Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about no deposit bonuses

Are no deposit bonuses really free?

Yes, no deposit bonuses are completely free to claim and don't require any initial deposit. However, you'll need to meet wagering requirements before withdrawing any winnings, and most casinos require a minimum deposit to process withdrawals.

Can I really win real money with no deposit bonuses?

Absolutely! Many players have won and successfully withdrawn real money from no deposit bonuses. The key is choosing bonuses with fair wagering requirements and following all terms and conditions carefully.

How long do I have to use a no deposit bonus?

Most no deposit bonuses have validity periods ranging from 7 to 30 days. Some may have shorter periods (24-48 hours) while premium offers might give you up to 60 days. Always check the specific terms for each bonus.

Can I claim multiple no deposit bonuses?

You can claim no deposit bonuses from different casinos, but typically only one per casino per player/household. Creating multiple accounts at the same casino will result in account closure and forfeiture of all funds.

What documents do I need for withdrawal?

For withdrawals, you'll typically need: government-issued ID (passport/license), proof of address (utility bill/bank statement), and sometimes proof of payment method. Complete this verification early to avoid delays when withdrawing.
